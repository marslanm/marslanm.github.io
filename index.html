Muhammad Arslan Manzoor

Ph.D. (NLP) candidate, MBZUAI, Abu-Dhabi - Masters in AI from NJUST, Nanjing, China.

Masdar City, Abu Dhabi

Hi, I am Ph.D. candidate at Mohamed Bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI). I am working with Professor Preslav Nakov and Dr. Shangsong Liang.

We are engaged in Media Profiling to assess bias and factuality at the outlet level. Our primary focus is understanding the correlations between mainstream media channels. We leverage social, contextual, and graphical data to predict credibility and political leanings. As this field is still emerging and relatively unexplored, our goal is to develop an efficient methodology capable of predicting media factuality and bias in real-time as soon as it’s published, without relying heavily on extensive data crawling, but rather by analyzing its position within the media landscape.

Before joining MBZUAI, I conducted research in Conversational AI for low-resource languages at Center for Language Engineering (CLE). In 2017, I was awarded the prestigious CSC scholarship for research based Master’s degree, which I completed in 2020 with a focus on Artificial Intelligence. During my research, I worked with Professor Jianfeng Lu on conducting sentiment analysis for low-resource language and code-switched data.
